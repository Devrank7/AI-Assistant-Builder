Elaine served as a Senior Sales and Marketing Consultant at Alternatives from November 2001 to June 2005 and held various roles within Procter &amp; Gamble&#x27;s Johnson Brothers Sales division from January 1997 to May 1999, including Senior Business Manager and Head of Greengage. In June 2005, Elaine founded Connolly Coaching &amp; Consulting, focusing on developing high performing teams for Irish SMEs and MNCs. Additionally, Elaine has contributed as a Team &amp; Leadership Coach in the MBA Programme at Trinity College Dublin since January 2019 and has been an Executive &amp; Leadership Coach at Tallaght University Hospital since June 2015. Elaine&#x27;s educational background includes a Masters in Business Studies from UCD Michael Smurfit Graduate Business School, a Post Graduate Diploma in Executive and Leadership Coaching, and a Diploma in Neuro Linguistic Programming.
