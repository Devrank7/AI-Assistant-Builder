## What Is Property Snagging

Property snagging is the process of identifying defects before you accept handover of a property. A "snag" can be any construction defect or quality issue including:

- Cracks in walls
- Poor tiling
- Leaking faucets
- Faulty electrical connections
- Hollow flooring
- Low AC cooling efficiency
- Damaged joinery
- Water pressure issues
- Faulty bathroom drainage
- Thermal insulation problems

Snagging ensures the developer — not you — pays for fixing these issues. It is like having your own personal engineer safeguard your investment.
