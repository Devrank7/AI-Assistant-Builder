## Focusing-Oriented Coaching Supervision

Services Focusing-Oriented Coaching Focusing-Oriented Coaching Supervision Focusing-Oriented Coaching Training Programs Book a Discovery Call Resources Research About News Menu Menu Focusing-Oriented Supervision Focusing-Oriented Coaching Supervision creates a safe and collaborative space for you to reflect on your clients, on yourself as a coach and on your coaching practice. Supervision is a place to unburdened, to learn, develop your reflective capacity, build your self-awareness and renew your energy for the benefit of yourself, your client and the overall system. Through reflective dialogue, you share what is going well, what you would like to change and what you notice in and about your coaching work, as you are gently invited to your learning edge. Focusing-Oriented Supervision is a supervision approach in which you will be oriented towards your felt experience of your coaching work. In Focusing-Oriented Supervision, you are supported to connect inward and articulate your in-the-moment experience in relation to your supervision question, so you can more clearly understand your client, yourself as coach and what is happening in the interaction between you both. Focusing-Oriented Supervision is a very powerful way of working and can constitute a full or be used as part of a Coaching supervision session. From here you begin to see more than before and unravel complexities, as your self-understanding supports you to become fully client centered, understanding how you, as coach may unknowingly be intruding on your client’s capacity to find their own answers. This approach brings new perspectives, fresh insights, clarity and an energy renewal that supports you to become masterful in your coaching work. With focusing, supervision is deeper and richer as you are gifted with a greater understanding of yourself and your clients as well as an invaluable skill to share with your coaching clients.
