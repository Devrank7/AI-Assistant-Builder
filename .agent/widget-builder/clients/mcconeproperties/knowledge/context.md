=== DUBAI REAL ESTATE FAQ ===

**Q: How can I find good real estate agents in Dubai?**
A: The best way is to ask for referrals from friends, family, and colleagues. Research agents who are experts in the local property sector and check their online reviews. You can also contact the Dubai Land Department for a list of licensed agents. McCone Properties is a top-rated, award-winning agency.

**Q: Can I rent a property in Dubai?**
A: Yes. Expats may rent or purchase residential properties in Dubai with valid proof of residency. McCone Properties helps tenants through the entire renting process with options for luxury or affordability.

**Q: Can I get a mortgage to buy property in Dubai?**
A: Yes. UAE nationals, expat residents, and non-resident international investors can obtain home loans in Dubai with necessary proof of income and documentation.

**Q: What are the best places to buy property in Dubai?**
A: Popular areas include Downtown Dubai, Dubai Marina, Dubai Hills Estate, Dubai Creek Harbour, Palm Jumeirah, Business Bay, Jumeirah Village Circle, and Arabian Ranches.

**Q: How much does a property cost in Dubai?**
A: Prices vary by location, size, and amenities. McCone Properties helps buyers find properties that match their budget and needs across all price ranges.

**Q: How to list property for sale in Dubai?**
A: You need to sign Form A (Contract A) with a broker and submit copies of your title deed/oqood, passport, and Emirates ID to the Dubai Land Department. The broker then applies for a marketing license.

=== BUYING GUIDE (DUBAI) ===

Key steps for buying property in Dubai:

1. Define your budget and preferred areas
2. Get mortgage pre-approval (if applicable)
3. Search for properties with a RERA-registered agent (like McCone Properties)
4. View properties and make an offer
5. Sign the MOU (Memorandum of Understanding)
6. Obtain a No Objection Certificate (NOC) from the developer
7. Transfer ownership at the Dubai Land Department
8. Register the property and receive the title deed

=== RENTING GUIDE (DUBAI) ===

Key steps for renting property in Dubai:

1. Define your budget and preferred location
2. Search for available properties
3. View properties and negotiate terms
4. Sign the tenancy contract
5. Register with Ejari (Dubai's official rental registration system)
6. Pay security deposit and rent
7. Set up DEWA (utilities) in your name
