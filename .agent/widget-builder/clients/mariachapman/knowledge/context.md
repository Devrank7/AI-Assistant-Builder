## About

About Work with me Contact About Work with me Contact Let's Talk Hi, I’m Maria A Cork girl, living in Dublin and mom to 3 smallies. I bring 15 years’ corporate leadership experience to my coaching practice and in 2010 I obtained my qualifications as a life and career coach. Fast forward to 2019 I made a deliberate career shift to recalibrate my life and I established my own coaching practice. I have always been massively interested in people, what motivates us and how we work. My focus as a coach lies in supporting individuals experiencing the weight of overwork, unfulfillment and a sense of disconnection from themselves. I am passionate about supporting clients to find themselves again and bring balance and joy back into their lives and careers. &nbsp; My approach includes a compassionate and holistic blend of psychological methods and personal development insights. If you find yourself feeling stuck in your career, looking for fulfillment or out of touch with yourself , then you’re in the right place.
