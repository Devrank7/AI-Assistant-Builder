## FAQ

**Q: What areas in Dubai do you specialize in?**
A: We cover all major Dubai areas including Business Bay, Downtown, JVC, World Islands, Dubai Marina, Palm Jumeirah, Dubailand, and more. We also serve Abu Dhabi and Sharjah.

**Q: Do you offer guaranteed ROI properties?**
A: Yes, we have exclusive partnerships offering guaranteed ROI of 8-10% annually for up to 10 years on select properties.

**Q: Can foreigners buy property in Dubai?**
A: Yes, foreigners can buy freehold property in designated areas across Dubai with full ownership rights.

**Q: What is the minimum investment amount?**
A: Investment properties start from approximately 770,000 AED (around $210,000 USD).

**Q: Do you help with mortgage/financing?**
A: We can connect you with our banking partners for mortgage pre-approval and financing options.

**Q: Can I get a UAE visa through property investment?**
A: Yes, property investment of 750,000 AED or above qualifies for a UAE Golden Visa (10-year residency).

**Q: How do I book a consultation?**

**Q: Is the consultation free?**
A: Yes, we offer free initial consultations with our senior property consultants.
