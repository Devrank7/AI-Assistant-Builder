# SPVC Real Estate Valuation Services Co. LLC

## About

SPVC Real Estate Valuation Services Co LLC is a Dubai Land Department (DLD) and RERA accredited valuation company. SPVC is registered with RERA for real estate valuation services and property observer activities.

SPVC marks its name in providing end-to-end quality service with a quick turnaround time, handled by trusted, experienced, and qualified professionals.

The parent company, Stiffeners Property Valuers and Consultants, is a South Asian valuation company operating across South India with 13 offices and over 150 employees, providing valuation services to more than 40 financial institutions, including nationalized banks, private banks, and NBFCs.

Mr. Mohamed Abdul Rahim is the Director of the company, with two decades of experience in valuation and building survey, heading both Stiffeners Property Valuers and Consultants and SPVC Real Estate Valuation Services Co. LLC. He is a Chartered Valuation Surveyor and RICS Registered Valuer. The parent company is also regulated by RICS, a globally recognized professional body.

### Property Observer Services

SPVC is registered with RERA for property observer activities and offers the following services:

- **Reserve Fund Study** - Comprehensive analysis of reserve fund requirements for property management
- **Building Condition Assessment (BCA)** - Thorough inspection and reporting on building conditions
- **Building Completion Audit** - Verification of construction completion standards
- **Snagging Report** - Detailed identification of defects and issues in new or existing properties
- **Service Charge Cost Allocation** - Fair distribution analysis of service charges
- **Asset Tagging Services** - Systematic asset identification and tracking
- **Asset Verification Approval Reports** - Verification and documentation of assets
