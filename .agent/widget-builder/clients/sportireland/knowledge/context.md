Executive Coaching with Maria Lynch and Nadine McCarthy Coach Profiles Maria Lynch Maria's background is in Business and Marketing with more than 15 years commercial experience working for multinationals in Ireland and abroad. In 2007 Maria embarked on a career transition to start her own business: MJL- Motivated Joyful Living- to dedicate herself to training and self-development for corporate and individual clients working as an Executive &amp; Confidence coach, trainer, and workshop facilitator. She is a Licensed NLP and EFT/TAT Master practitioner and Image Consultant. As a professional trainer Maria holds an IITD qualification in Training &amp; Development and holds a BA in Business and a Master of Science in Marketing Practice. She has extensive experience delivering training &amp; coaching programmes in Personal development and Essential Business Management Skills to a broad spectrum of clients such as The Irish Sports Council, KPMG, Emmaus Retreat Centre, IBM, Enterprise Women Network, FAS, Cork Chambers Business School, Galway ITAG, Dublin Enterprise board and more.Maria is originally from Chile and brings an open, creative, practical, and energetic approach to her work. Her personality is down to earth and she puts participants at ease sharing her knowledge in a positive environment.
