About LEA LEA Consulting Limited is a Consulting Business aimed at the provision of Expert and Legal Advocacy to the Construction Industry. Our goal is to help our Clients manage their risk in the key areas of; Contract Negotiation, Commercial Management &amp; Control, Claims Preparation &amp; Defence, Dispute Resolution and Expert Services. This is achieved through the development of enduring partnerships with our Clients and the provision of a one to one service, that allows LEA, with our Clients, to identify risk at the earliest possible stage, design strategies to manage risks identified and assist in the implementation of those strategies to ensure that the impact of such risks on our Client’s businesses are eliminated, or mitigated to the extent possible. Ian O’Connor B.Sc.(Hons), LLM CLArb, MCIArb (Med), CMMII, Certified Mediator Ian is LEA’s founder and Lead Consultant, his reputation is one of a driven professional, with a no-nonsense approach, a skilled negotiator, with a flair for developing creative solutions to complex Contractual problems. Ian’s experience of the Construction Industry has been based in tier one Main Contracting allowing him, or two decades, to interact and work with blue chip clients, tier one Design Teams and the entire spectrum of Sub-Contractors / Suppliers, making him extremely familiar to those operating in the industry and ensuring that his knowledge of the industry is second to none.
