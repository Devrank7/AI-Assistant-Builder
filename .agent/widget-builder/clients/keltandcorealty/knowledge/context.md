=== FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS ===

**Q: Can foreigners buy property in Dubai?**
A: Yes, foreigners can buy property in designated freehold areas in Dubai. There are no restrictions on nationality, and buyers receive full ownership rights.

**Q: What is an off-plan property?**
A: Off-plan properties are developments that are sold before or during construction. They typically offer lower prices, flexible payment plans, and potential for capital appreciation by the time of handover.

**Q: What are the typical payment plans?**
A: Most off-plan properties in Dubai offer payment plans such as 60/40 or 45/55, where you pay a percentage during construction and the remainder upon handover. Some developers offer post-handover payment plans as well.

**Q: What is a Golden Visa?**
A: The UAE Golden Visa is a long-term residency visa (5 or 10 years) available to property investors who purchase real estate worth AED 2 million or more.
