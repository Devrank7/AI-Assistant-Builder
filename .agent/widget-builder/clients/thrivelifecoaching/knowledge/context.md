## About Me

Skip to content Menu Home Self-Empowerment About Me Qualifications Blog (Journal) FAQ Contact Us About Me Why Choose Us I am a husband of twenty-five years to my incredible wife and a father to three wonderful young adults. I am a son, a brother, an uncle, and a friend to many. My journey of self-discovery led me to the profound questions: “Who am I?” and “Who am I beyond my relationship with others?” This inquiry catalysed my transition into Life Coaching. Born in Tipperary, I ventured away from home at eighteen to pursue my studies in Galway, where I earned an Honours Degree in Civil Engineering. In 2002, I co-founded a civil engineering company with my brother Mark. We thrived together for seven years. However, in 2009, the global economic downturn brought our venture to an end. The loss was significant, as I had poured my heart and soul into the business. It was during this challenging time that I discovered meditation, a practice that helped me transcend my overthinking mind. I learned to observe my thoughts rather than be consumed by them, exploring their origins and meanings. This journey led me to the teachings of Hinduism and Buddhism, inspired by Michael Singer’s The Untethered Soul. As the years passed, I balanced family life, meditation, training, and work. The pandemic marked a turning point in my life. While many faced confinements, I remained engaged in a project deemed of “vital national importance.” However, I found myself at a crossroads, reflecting on the swift passage of time as I entered my 50s and my daughter turned 20. I began to question my long-term commitment to the construction industry and felt a strong desire to contribute more meaningfully to the world. Inspired by an exercise from Tim Ferriss, I envisioned myself on my deathbed, contemplating my life. Was I content with how I had spent my time? ## FAQ

Skip to content Menu Home Self-Empowerment About Me Qualifications Blog (Journal) FAQ Contact Us FAQs What is Life Coaching? Life Coaching is all about unlocking your own potential. It focuses on empowering you to find your own solutions and take ownership of your own growth. It is a form of learning. The purpose of a life coach is to kickstart your journey and assist you along the way. How can Life Coaching help me? If you've been searching for a way to reach your potential, solve a problem or create change in your life, Life Coaching can help you move forward. It can open up discussions that increase your self-awareness and help you to assess the choices available to you. What areas of life can you help me with? I can help you with any of the following and more: Work-Life Balance Career Professional Development Relationships Time Management Goal Setting In a broader sense, my goal is to help you find your Life Purpose. What qualifications do you hold? I am a fully qualified Life Coach with a Professional Diploma in Coaching &amp; Mentoring from Kingstown College in Dun Laoghaire, as well as a Precision Nutrition Qualified Coach. I am also a degree-qualified engineer, and have worked in the construction industry for over 30 years. How does the process work? Initially, we set up a discovery call (free of charge) to establish what you want, and most importantly, if I'm the right coach for you. Our first official meeting will then help me to understand your current situation and goals, and in the regular meetings that follow we will set objectives and create an action plan together, exploring any challenges you face along the way. How often and where do meetings take place? Timing can vary, but ideally, we would have a minimum one-hour session every two weeks. Meetings can be hosted virtually or in my home office in Dublin.
