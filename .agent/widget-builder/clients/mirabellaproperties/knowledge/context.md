=== ABOUT JUMEIRAH GOLF ESTATES ===

Jumeirah Golf Estates is Dubai's premier golf estate destination. Living in JGE feels like stepping into a quieter, greener side of the city without leaving Dubai behind. Open skies, manicured fairways and quiet streets come together naturally.

Set across wide green spaces, JGE offers a lifestyle that suits families, professionals and long-term investors. Residents wake up to tree-lined views, enjoy peaceful walks, and still reach key business hubs with ease.

At its heart are two championship golf courses — Fire and Earth — designed by Greg Norman. The community features:

- Landscaped parks and shaded play areas
- Walking and cycling tracks across neighbourhoods
- Schools and childcare options within easy reach
- Clubhouse life that brings neighbours together
- Quick access to major roads, connecting to Dubai Marina, Downtown and airports

JGE is a freehold property area, meaning international buyers can own with full rights.

**Service Charges**: Range from AED 4-5 per square foot (set by WASL Properties, the master developer). Issued once a year to homeowners, calculated based on built-up area (BUA).

**Clubhouse Membership**: Mandatory AED 12,500 per house. Al Andalus residents get optional membership at AED 12,500 per couple, AED 1,000 per child, AED 3,000 for additional adults.

**Operator**: WASL Properties owns and operates JGE. WASL Community Management issues service charge invoices.

**Regulatory Authority**: Jumeirah Golf Estates falls under Trakhees PCFC.

=== MORTGAGE INFORMATION ===

Mirabella Properties connects buyers with trusted third-party mortgage specialists to find competitive rates across the UAE.

**Borrowing Limit**: Typically capped at 7x the applicant's annual salary (varies by bank). Existing loans, credit cards, and liabilities impact the maximum.

**Interest Rates**: Vary significantly from bank to bank, based on individual profile (salaried vs self-employed).

**Non-Resident Financing**: Available with maximum loan-to-value (LTV) up to 60%, interest rates starting from 3.89% for 3-year fixed term.

**Minimum Down Payment:**

- First-time buyers: Expat residents 20%, UAE nationals 15%
- Second-time buyers: Expat residents 40%, UAE nationals 35%
- Some banks offer reduced rates (20% expat, 15% UAE national) for second-time buyers if existing mortgage LTV is below 60%

**Required Documents (Salaried):**

- Identification documents
- Salary certificate
- Last 6 months' payslips
- Last 6 months' personal bank statements
- Details of existing liabilities

**Required Documents (Self-Employed):**

- Company documents (trade licence, incorporation docs, MOA, shareholder certificate, company profile)
- Audited financial statements and/or 12 months' business bank statements

**Pre-Approval Timeline**: Initial document requests within 2 business days. Pre-approval generally issued within 5-7 business days after all documents submitted.
