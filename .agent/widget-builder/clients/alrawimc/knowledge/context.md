# AlRawi Medical Center (مركز الراوي الطبي)

## About

AlRawi Medical Center is a premium medical and dental clinic located in City Walk, Dubai. Established in 2016 by Mr. Talal AlRawi, the center specializes in dental treatments, beauty services, injectables, and skin tightening.

Their approach focuses on results, research, development, and superior client service. They hold themselves to the highest professional and safety standards to deliver results that are noticeable but natural-looking. Each patient is treated as a unique individual, with treatment plans tailored specifically for them.

The clinic offers a wide range of treatment options, combining depth of experience, medical understanding, and state-of-the-art equipment.

**Tagline:** "Perfecting your beauty is our passion & mission"
