## Services

### 1. Newly Handed Over Properties Inspection

- **Purpose:** Identify construction defects before the developer's defect liability period ends.
- **Why it's critical:** Free rectifications are available within a limited time.
- **Service Focus:** Identifying all construction flaws and systems that do not meet local regulations or specifications.
- **Key Benefit:** Ensures a smooth, stress-free handover process and protects you from incurring future repair costs.

### 2. End-of-Defect Liability Period (DLP) Inspection (11-Month Warranty Inspection)

- **Purpose:** Performed near the end of the warranty period to maximize your claim against the developer.
- **Service Focus:** Checking for issues that may have developed or become apparent since handover such as settlement cracks and latent defects.
- **Key Benefit:** Uses your final opportunity to legally compel the developer to rectify latent defects before the warranty expires.

### 3. Resale & Pre-Purchase Inspection (Secondary Market Properties)

- **Purpose:** Pre-purchase inspection for an informed investment.
- **Service Focus:** Assessing the age, wear, maintenance condition, and operational status of all major systems and structural elements.
- **Key Benefit:** Provides crucial negotiation leverage by giving you an accurate picture of expected maintenance costs.

### 4. Post-Renovation & Contractor Work Inspection

- **Purpose:** Verify renovation quality before final payment or move-in.
- **Service Focus:** Checking the workmanship, material quality, and adherence to design specifications for all recent works.
- **Key Benefit:** Guarantees that you only sign off on and pay for work that is completed correctly and professionally.

### 5. Rental Properties Inspection

- **Purpose:** Ensure the property is safe, functional, and rentable.
- **Why it's critical:** Prevent complaints and protect rental value.

### 6. Commercial Properties Inspection

- **Purpose:** Verify compliance and usability for retail, office, or warehouse use.
- **Why it's critical:** Avoid business disruptions and liabilities.

## Scope of Work (What We Inspect)

### Electrical Fittings & Systems

- Switches and sockets alignment

- Circuit breaker (DB) panel check

### Painting & Wall Finishes

- Paint uniformity and touch-ups

- Cooling performance test
- Thermostat functionality

- Water pressure and flow

- Proper opening/closing

## Advanced Tools & Technology

### 1. Thermal Imaging (Infrared) Cameras

- Detects temperature variations across surfaces (non-destructive testing)
- Reveals hidden moisture and leaks behind walls
- Identifies missing/damaged insulation and energy loss
- Detects electrical hotspots, overloaded circuits, and fire risks
- Critical for waterproofing inspection

### 2. Digital Moisture Meters

- Measures percentage of water content within building materials
- Both pin-type and non-invasive scanning meters
- Confirms and quantifies severity of moisture detected by thermal camera
- Provides quantitative proof for snagging reports

### 3. High-Precision Laser Measuring Tools

- Checks if walls are plumb (perfectly vertical), floors are level
- Verifies room sizes, ceiling heights match developer floor plans
- Measures alignment and tolerance with engineering precision

### 4. Anemometers & HVAC Performance Testers

- Measures velocity and volume of air flow from HVAC vents
- Tests air supply rate to ensure correct cooling capacity
- Checks suction power of exhaust fans in kitchens/bathrooms

### 5. Inspection Cameras (Borescopes & Drones)

- Drones for aerial inspection of high-rise facades, roofs, and compounds
- Borescopes (snake cameras) for wall cavities, AC ducts, and plumbing voids
- Ensures 100% inspection coverage

### 6. Spectora Software

- Industry-leading inspection reporting software
- Generates precise, visual, photo-supported reports
- Modern solution built for performance, trust, and usability
- From inspection to report delivery

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What is property snagging and why do I need it?**
A: Property snagging is a detailed inspection of a newly handed-over or existing property to identify defects, incomplete works, or safety issues. It helps you get defects fixed by the developer or contractor before they become costly problems.

**Q: How long does an inspection take?**
A: Typical inspections take between 1-4 hours depending on the property size and type. We provide a clear estimate during booking.

**Q: Will I receive a report after the inspection?**
A: Yes — clients receive a photo-supported, severity-graded snagging report suitable for submission to developers and contractors. Reports are typically delivered within 12 hours.

**Q: Do you cover all emirates in the UAE?**
A: Yes — we operate across Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Sharjah, Ras Al Khaimah, and other UAE emirates. Contact us to confirm availability in your area.

**Q: How do I book an inspection?**
A: You can book online using our booking form at mypropertysnagging.com/booking, call us at +971 55 412 1205, or use WhatsApp.

**Q: What property types do you inspect?**
A: We inspect villas/townhouses, apartments, and commercial properties including retail, office, and warehouse spaces.

**Q: What is a Defect Liability Period (DLP)?**
A: Most new properties come with a standard Defect Liability Period (warranty). Our 11-month warranty inspection is performed near the end of this period to maximize your claim against the developer before the warranty expires.

**Q: How much does snagging cost?**
A: Pricing depends on the property type and size. Contact us for an instant quote via our website, WhatsApp, or phone. Provide your property type (Villa/Townhouse, Apartment, or Commercial), built-up size, number of bedrooms, and project name for an accurate quote.
