=== VALUATION PROCESS ===

Step 1: Preparation of Required Documents

- Request documents such as title deeds, architectural plans, legal permits, and any other documents related to the property
- Review documents to ensure accuracy and completeness

Step 2: Data Collection

- Conduct an on-site inspection of the property to assess its actual condition and gather necessary data
- Take detailed notes and photographs of the interior and exterior
- Verify the dimensions and areas mentioned in the documents

Step 3: Market Analysis

- Gather data on comparable properties in the area in terms of size, location, and features
- Analyze supply and demand
- Review sale and rental prices of similar properties

Step 4: Property Valuation

- Use valuation methods such as the sales comparison approach, cost approach, and income approach
- Estimate market value — determine the current market value of the property based on data and analysis

Step 5: Issuing Fair Valuation

- Write a comprehensive appraisal report that includes all details related to the property, the valuation methodology used, and the analyses and results
- Review the report to ensure information and valuations are accurate and correct
- Deliver the report to the client
- Discuss results, explain valuations, and answer any questions

Turnaround Time: Certificates issued in 24-48 business hours
