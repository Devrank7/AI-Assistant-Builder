## Valuation Methodologies

Western Valuers employs five recognized valuation approaches:

1. **The Comparable Approach (Market Approach)** - Comparing the subject property to similar properties of known value, making allowances for differences.

2. **The Investment Approach (Income Approach)** - Capital value relates directly to the income generated or expected. Considers quantum of income, quality of security, duration, and future trends.

3. **The Residual Approach** - Used for building land/development property, estimating output value minus input costs (site preparation, building costs, fees, finance charges).

4. **The Profit Approach** - For trade-related properties where land and building are valued together with the business (e.g., hotels, pubs, petrol stations).

5. **The Cost Approach** - Based on replacement cost principle, commonly used for new or unique properties.
