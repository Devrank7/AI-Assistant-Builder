## Dubai Area Guides

### Dubai Water Canal

A 3.2 km man-made waterway connecting Dubai Creek to the Arabian Gulf. Surrounded by landmarks like the St. Regis and W Dubai, it features luxury residences, leisure spots, and scenic walkways — a prime waterfront destination.

### City Walk, Dubai

Developed by MERAAS, City Walk is a vibrant open-air lifestyle destination combining upscale living, shopping, dining, and entertainment. Spanning over 10 million sq. ft., it blends modern residential and commercial spaces with a focus on design, community, and urban connectivity.

### Downtown Dubai

The vibrant heart of the city. Centrally located, home to iconic landmarks like the Burj Khalifa, Dubai Mall, and the Dubai Fountain. Offers luxury residences, world-class dining, and endless entertainment — one of the most prestigious and dynamic communities in Dubai.

### Jumeirah Park

By Nakheel, a 370-hectare residential community featuring around 2,000 luxury villas in heritage, regional, and rustic styles. Known for its family-friendly environment with landscaped parks, green spaces, and a peaceful suburban lifestyle.
