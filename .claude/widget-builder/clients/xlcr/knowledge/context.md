## About Us

XLCR Real Estate (Pronounced Excelsior) is a boutique property company based in Dubai; providing brokerage and consulting services on realty projects throughout the UAE. Formed in 2021, the two co-founders collectively possess nearly 50 years real estate experience, each of whom have resided and sold property in Dubai since 2008 and 2010.

Throughout this 14 and 12 year period both Lyndsey and Nick have forged long-term relationships with buyers, sellers and property developers; resulting in a market-wide network. This significant level of intellectual property can only be gained with tenure, and is consequently put to exclusive use for XLCR's client base.

### Connecting Those Dots

The people who are XLCR have created, nurtured and developed substantial relationships with key, senior individuals within the region's most established and reputable property developers. These relationships, which often result in exclusive and early access to new property launches, are the added value XLCR can provide. A deep, yet broad, understanding of the property market is required when consulting to a buyer who is overseas or new to the Dubai real estate market. An intrinsic ability to understand our buyers' requirements, and present multiple options from a suite of possibilities, ensures our valued and longstanding client base continue to engage our services and refer the same to their friends and colleagues.

### Our Mandate

The mandate of XLCR is very simple, to deliver excellence. In a marketplace where competitor real estate companies value their headcount, and pace at which they can grow their team, as their key success barometer; XLCR views success differently. XLCR has a desired company structure of very experienced consultants who manage an existing portfolio of established clients; consulting not only to their buying needs but also their divestment and risk management requirements.
