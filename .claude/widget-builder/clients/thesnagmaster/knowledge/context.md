## Services

### 1. Residential Property Inspection

Protect your investment with expert residential property inspections by qualified engineers. Detailed move-in, move-out, and handover inspections, plus resale, fitout, DLP, MEP, and water damage assessments. Available for villas, apartments, and townhouses across the UAE.

**Residential Inspection Types:**

#### Handover Inspection

Detailed handover inspections to identify various defects (Civil + MEP) before you officially take possession. Engineers ensure your property meets quality standards, highlighting issues for rectification.

#### Resale Property Inspection

Resale/secondary purchase inspections help buyers and sellers uncover hidden defects in secondary properties. Detailed reports provide a clear picture of the property's condition, helping negotiate better deals.

#### Move-In/Out Inspection

Move-in/move-out inspections to document property conditions before and after tenancy. Protects landlords and tenants by minimizing disputes over damages. Comprehensive reports ensure a hassle-free rental or handover experience.

#### Interior Fitout Inspection

Interior fit out inspections ensure all finishes, fittings, and mechanical installations meet design and quality standards. Checks for workmanship defects, incomplete installations, and compliance issues.

#### Defect Liability Period (DLP) Inspection

DLP inspections help owners identify various defects (Civil + MEP) before the warranty period ends. Detailed reports assist in requesting necessary repairs from developers before coverage expires.

#### MEP Inspection

Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing (MEP) inspections to assess critical systems' performance and safety. Verifies correct installation, compliance, and operational integrity for long-term reliability.

#### Property Management Inspection

Supports property managers or property management companies with routine inspections to monitor maintenance standards, tenant-related damages, and property conditions.

#### Water Damage Inspection

Water damage inspections detect leaks, moisture infiltration, and plumbing issues using advanced tools. Identifies sources of water damage early, protecting property structure and maintaining a healthy, safe living environment.

## Inspection Checkpoints (Scope of Inspection)

### HVAC

Inspect air conditioning system, water heater, ventilation ducts, and electrical connections to identify issues affecting cooling, heating, airflow, or water flow.

### Plumbing Systems

Inspect supply lines, traps, waste pipes, faucets, and other plumbing components to identify potential leaks, clogs, and corrosion.

### Electrical Components

Inspect the main electrical panel, circuit load, sockets, wiring, switches, and light fixtures to ensure compliance with UAE electrical standards and reduce the risk of short circuits.

### Doors/Windows Insulation

Inspect door and window installations for proper insulation, alignment, sealing, and functionality.

### Garden/Garage (Exterior)

Inspect drainage systems, water run-off, manhole locations, driveways, pathways, and patio surfaces as part of external property inspection.

### Kitchen/Bathroom

Inspect all worktops, surfaces, cupboards, door alignments, sanitaryware, shower fittings, screens, and floor levels.

### Walls and Floor Finishing

Inspect walls, floors, and ceilings to identify structural or non-structural cracks, poor paint finishes, and hidden stains.

### Moisture Content

Use advanced thermal imaging and moisture detection tools to spot hidden leaks, mold, and other issues.

## FAQs

**Q: What is property snagging and why is it important?**
A: A property inspection is a detailed check of a home or building to find defects, safety issues, or poor workmanship. It helps buyers, owners, and tenants understand the real condition of the property before moving in or making payments.

**Q: When should I schedule a snagging inspection in Dubai?**
A: You should book a snagging inspection before handover or right after construction is completed. It ensures all defects are found early and fixed by the developer before you move in.

**Q: How long does a home inspection take?**
A: A standard home inspection takes about 1 to 3 hours, depending on the size and type of the property. Larger villas/townhouses may take longer.

**Q: What will I receive after the inspection?**
A: You will get a detailed inspection report with photos/videos, notes, and a list of all identified issues within 24 to 48 hours. This report can be used to request repairs or for future reference.

**Q: What is a De-Snagging inspection?**
A: De-Snagging is a follow-up inspection done after the developer has fixed the issues found in the first snagging report. It checks whether the defects have been properly repaired before you accept the handover.

**Q: How much does a property inspection cost in Dubai?**
A: Inspection prices in the UAE are based on the size of the property. Competitive pricing is tailored to specific requirements. Please contact for more information.

**Q: Is property snagging only for new properties?**
A: No, property snagging is useful for both new and secondary (resale) properties. In resale units, it helps check for hidden damages, poor repairs, or wear and tear before you buy. Also applicable for properties with newly done interior-fitout, water damage, mold formation, etc.

**Q: How experienced are your property snagging engineers?**
A: The Snag Master is a RERA-certified company. All inspections are carried out by qualified civil engineers with over 5 years of experience in UAE property construction.

**Q: Do you conduct property snagging in Abu Dhabi, Sharjah, and other Emirates?**
A: Yes, we offer property snagging and inspection services across all Emirates, including Abu Dhabi, Sharjah, Dubai, Ajman, and more. Our team travels to your location for a detailed and professional inspection.
