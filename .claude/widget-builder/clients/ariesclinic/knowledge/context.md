# Aries Aesthetic and Dental Clinic

## About

Aries Aesthetic and Dental Clinic is a premium multi-specialty clinic located in Dubai, UAE. The clinic combines advanced aesthetic treatments with comprehensive dental care, offering patients a luxurious, all-in-one destination for beauty and dental wellness. The clinic is licensed under MOHAP (Ministry of Health and Prevention), license number W1LUAGE8-171025.

At Aries, the story is one of passion, perseverance, and a shared vision. The journey began with the desire to create a haven where aesthetics and dental care converge, providing the highest quality treatments in a warm and welcoming environment.
